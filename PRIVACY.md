# ReadEase Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 12, 2026*

ReadEase does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. There are no
analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no servers. The extension makes zero
network requests.

## What the extension stores

- **Per-site text-size settings** (the scale percentage and the main-text /
  whole-page choice) and **your highlight color** are saved with
  `chrome.storage.sync`. This data stays inside your browser profile; if you
  have Chrome sync enabled, Chrome may sync it between your own devices
  through your Google account, like any other extension setting. It is never
  sent to the developer or any third party.
- **Highlights** exist only in the page you made them on and disappear when
  the page is closed or reloaded. They are not stored.

## What the extension reads

To resize text and place highlights, the content script runs on pages you
visit and works with the page's text locally, in your browser. Page content
is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.
